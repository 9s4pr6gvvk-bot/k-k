iii BIM — Building Information Modelling
DigitalCoordination

When did it all begin
For over 15 years Knapton and Knapton has developed the use of Building Information Modelling software to deliver projects. We now use this software platform to deliver all projects within the office, even in situations where the client has not requested BIM it is our preferred software platform.

What does it mean
Throughout this time we have become market leaders with our considerable knowledge, intellect and expertise in the use and application of BIM software in the working environment. Some people mistakenly think of BIM as just a new variety of software drawing package. BIM is actually a process that relies on information-rich models. Project teams can use information contained in the BIM models to perform a variety of complementary tasks, including energy or environmental analysis, visualization, construction simulation and improving the accuracy of documentation.

Value added
Early access to the rich information in the models helps stakeholders on the project team gain more insight into their projects. As a result, the team can make more informed decisions much earlier in the planning, design, construction, or renovation process when decisions can have the greatest impact on project cost, schedule, and sustainability. To realise these benefits it is imperative that BIM standards are set up early and accurately and adhered to.

What we do well

  • Content creation — Families and Materials
  • Training and support
  • Advice on hardware requirements
  • Partnering solutions with clients on project delivery

iv V.R. — Virtual Reality
VisualisationClient Experience

Why V.R.? Virtual Reality
The introduction of the V.R. platform within the company was born out of a need for us to be able to give our clients a high level of 3D visual information that we had access to via the BIM platform. The problem with the BIM platform is the specialist nature of the operating hardware, and we needed to find a 3D software system that was less demanding.

How it works
Using the BIM 3D model created for Planning and construction provides opportunities for better understanding of projects through accurate technical 3D visualisation. Through the development of a software bridge we are able to import technically accurate models into the V.R. package, allowing us to create accessible real time 3D models for our clients. This allows the client to explore the 3D environment in real time with ease.

What are the results?
VR software, unlike traditional visualisation methods provides the possibility of fast still and moving HD image production and even real time exploration of sites directly from the planning or construction information without the need for render farms and weeks of expensive computer processing time. This proves invaluable for project team members and stakeholders to gain a greater understanding of the project and spot issues or opportunities early in the process before it is too late to make amendments. Marketing teams benefit from a vast amount of 3D images and animations giving them the opportunity to engage at pre build stage with potential clients.
