We are an independent architecture practice based at Dean Clough Mills, Halifax since 1987. From sensitive heritage conservation to new civic commissions, we design buildings that genuinely belong to their place.

Three generations of craft and care inform every project. We know the grain, stone and character of West Yorkshire's built environment — and that knowledge shapes everything we do.
