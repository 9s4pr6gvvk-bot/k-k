When did it all beginFor over 15 years Knapton and Knapton has developed the use of Building Information Modelling software to deliver projects. We now use this software platform to deliver all projects within the office, even in situations where the client has not requested BIM it is our preferred software platform.

What does it meanThroughout this time we have become market leaders with our considerable knowledge, intellect and expertise in the use and application of BIM software in the working environment. BIM is actually a process that relies on information-rich models. Project teams can use information contained in the BIM models to perform a variety of complementary tasks, including energy or environmental analysis, visualisation, construction simulation and improving the accuracy of documentation.

Value addedEarly access to the rich information in the models helps stakeholders on the project team gain more insight into their projects. As a result, the team can make more informed decisions much earlier in the planning, design, construction, or renovation process when decisions can have the greatest impact on project cost, schedule, and sustainability.

Common Questions

A few things people usually ask before getting in touch about BIM.

What is BIM and why do you use it on every project?

BIM — Building Information Modelling — is a process built around information-rich 3D models rather than flat drawings. We've used it as our preferred software platform for over 15 years and apply it to every project, even where a client hasn't specifically asked for it.

Do I need to request BIM specifically, or is it already included?

BIM is already our default way of working — it's built into how we deliver every project rather than being an optional extra you need to ask for.

Can you create custom BIM content for our practice or product range?

Yes. Content creation — including bespoke families and materials — is one of the things we do well, alongside training, support and advice on hardware requirements.

Do you offer BIM training or support for our own team?

Yes, we offer training and support and can partner with clients directly on BIM-led project delivery.
